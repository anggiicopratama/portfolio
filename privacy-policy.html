Privacy Policy

Anggi Ico Pratama respects your privacy and is committed to keeping any information you share on this site safe and purposeful.

Information Collection

This personal site does not use registration forms. Any information you volunteer via email or the contact form is limited to the details you choose to provide.

How Information Is Used

Messages are used solely to respond to your inquiries, collaborate, or share updates you explicitly requested. Details are not sold or shared with third parties.

Cookies & Analytics

The main portfolio page uses basic analytics to understand visits and improve the experience. These analytics collect aggregate metrics and do not identify you personally.

Advertising & Third-Party Links

To keep this website sustainable, selected pages may display contextual ads or affiliate links. These partners may place cookies to measure performance. All partners are reviewed to ensure they comply with industry privacy standards.

  • No ad partner receives direct access to your messages or contact form submissions.
  • Outgoing affiliate links are clearly labeled so you know when a commission might be earned.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer not to be tracked.

Data Retention

Emails and form inputs are archived for up to 12 months to maintain project history and client references. After that, messages are deleted during scheduled cleanups unless a collaboration is ongoing.

Your Rights & Choices

You may request a copy of any personal data stored about you, ask for corrections, or demand deletion. Simply email the request and it will be processed within 14 days.

International Visitors

This site is hosted in Indonesia. If you are accessing it from another region, understand that your data will be processed under Indonesian law, which may differ from your local regulations.

Policy Updates

This policy is reviewed twice a year or whenever a new feature (newsletter, membership, etc.) is introduced. Significant changes will be highlighted on the homepage for transparency.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out via the contact page or email hello@anggicodes.test. Include the subject line “Privacy Request” for faster handling.
